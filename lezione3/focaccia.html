Focaccia whit Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipe That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off whit a paring knife if desired, tought it's not necessary)
  • 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
in a medium,sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tough I sametimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
